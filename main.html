About CanvasCraft

CanvasCraft is a versatile painting application that allows users to express their creativity through a variety of tools and features. Whether you are a professional artist or just someone who loves to doodle, CanvasCraft offers an intuitive interface and powerful functionality to bring your ideas to life.

With CanvasCraft, you can select colors, shapes, and even draw freely using a pencil option. The application supports touch events, making it perfect for use on tablets and mobile devices. You can also download your creations, upload images to the canvas, and share your artwork directly to Facebook.

One of the unique features of CanvasCraft is its ability to adjust the size of shapes dynamically based on the speed of your cursor, adding an extra layer of creativity and control to your artwork.
